    Splash CMS is a super simple, open source content management system for ColdFusion developers.

    Splash CMS features:

    • Simple, elegant interface
    • Flexible templating using layouts
    • Page and Snippet management
    • Custom tagging language called SplashTags to work with content
    • Simple user management

    Splash CMS is a content management system that is developed in ColdFusion and designed to be extremely simple and elegant. Yet allowing you the flexibility you need to build your site. It has none of the “fluff” of other CMS systems.

    You can find the latest documentation info on the wiki.

    http://wiki.github.com/russjohnson/SplashCMS

    Splash utilizes three main components when building a site. They are layouts, pages, and snippets. Everything in a Splash site is contained in those three components.

    Mailing List

    The quickest way to get help with Splash is to join the mailing list at google groups. You can post questions or suggestions and usually get a reply pretty quickly. You can even browse through the messages without signing up and search for answers to questions or issues that have already been posted.

    The traffic is low right now so you wont get flooded with emails just by signing up.
